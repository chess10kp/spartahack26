# Master Knowledge

## Overall Goal
Capture team ideas during the hackathon, summarize them, and guide the team toward the ideal project outcome.

## Core System
- Microphone captures team discussions and generates transcripts.
- Summarizer agent condenses transcripts into key points.
- Relevant summaries are stored in a vector database for retrieval.
- Off-track discussion should be redirected back to project goals.

## Orchestration
- Orchestrator assigns tasks based on client capabilities and load.
- Task Manager converts milestones into subtasks and updates a dependency graph.
- Timekeeper checks progress and reports pace.

## Success Criteria
- Reliable capture of ideas and decisions.
- Clear task breakdown with minimal blockers.
- Team stays aligned with goals.
